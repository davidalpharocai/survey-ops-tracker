# CCM cheat sheet — one page for the teach-in

**What it is:** AlphaROC's system of record for what clients **buy** (contracts
add credits/dollars) and **use** (studies consume them). Live at the link your
manager shared — sign in with your **@alpharoc.ai email** + the shared password.

---

### ▶ Record a study (the survey you delivered — spends the balance)
Home → **Record a Study**
1. Client → Contact(s) it's for → title → date
2. Cadence: **Single** (one-off) or **weekly/monthly/quarterly** (tracker)
3. Cost type (credits or dollars) → Cost
   - Single = total. Tracker = **per run** (billed × 52/12/4 a year) + optional one-time setup (credits).
4. Publish → the client's balance drops.

### ▶ Add a contract (what they bought — tops the balance up)
Home → **Add a Contract**
1. Client → title → date (renewal auto-fills +1 year)
2. Credits and/or dollars → Record → balance goes up.

### ▶ Look something up
- **Client Balances** — everyone's remaining credits/$ + next renewal. Click a client → full history → **Download PDF** statement.
- **Renewal Radar** — what's renewing in the next 30/60/90 days.
- **Balance Health** — who's burning fast / about to run dry.

---

### Rules that keep it clean
- **Search before adding a client** — no duplicates.
- **Attribute every study** to the contact who requested it.
- **Price studies promptly** — a 0-credit study is unbilled work.
- **Fixing a mistake?** Edit the row (it's tracked) or add an **Adjustment**
  (a correction line with a note) — never re-type history. "Delete" = archive;
  nothing is ever destroyed, and admins can restore it.

### Every field has an **(i)** — hover it if you're unsure. That's the whole app.
